What is ethical data?

 

What's ethical and what's not is a philosophical question. And, like all great philosophical questions, there's no final answer.  Instead of debating endlessly, we're going to do like the Greeks did. We're going to make our decisions democratically!!  

At Sfir, we believe there are no final answers when it comes to ethical data use. The only ethical policy, is one that lets you, the citizens of the Sfirocracy, be part of an ongoing process to decide how your data is used.
